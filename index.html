Wordle Clone

Guess the hidden word in 6 tries. A fun and addictive word-guessing game!

About Wordle Clone

Wordle Clone is an online word-guessing game where players have six attempts to guess a hidden five-letter word. Each guess provides feedback on which letters are correct and in the right position. It's a fun and challenging way to test your vocabulary and logic skills!

How to Play

  1. Enter a 5-letter word and press Enter.
  2. Letters in the correct position will turn green.
  3. Letters that are in the word but in the wrong position will turn yellow.
  4. Letters not in the word will remain gray.
  5. Use the feedback to guess the word within six attempts.

Why Play Wordle Clone?

Wordle Clone is a great way to improve your vocabulary, enhance your problem-solving skills, and have fun at the same time. It's perfect for word game enthusiasts and puzzle lovers!
